Step 2 — Create config.py implementing:
- `CONFIG_PATH = Path.home() / ".drillclips" / "config.json"`
- `class Settings(BaseSettings)` with:
  - `cache_dir: Path = Field(default_factory=lambda: Path.home() / ".drillclips" / "cache")`

- `class ConfigResponse(BaseModel)`: `cache_dir: Path`, `output_dir: Path`
- `class ConfigUpdate(BaseModel)`: `output_dir: Optional[Path] = None`
